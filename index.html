KSP delta v interactive map

This tool will estimate the delta v requirements to get around the Kerbal Universe. Right now this uses simple Hohmann transfers, and some overrides for planets with atmosphere.

Pinning: Click to unpin, pick a route and then pin again - now you can select destinations with a single click.

Values you can tweak:
  • Aero braking: assumes you have sufficient heat capacity / ablatives / parachutes.
  • Min braking dv: an additional flat rate cost for using burns to control your aero braking.
  • Plane changes: if you want to land on planetary equators, instead of 'anywhere', you'll want to take this into account.
Non-tweakable assumptions and their liability to over (+) or under (-) estimate:
  • (-) Some burns are assumed not to be circularised e.g. child soi -> parent orbit, child soi -> parent soi.
  • (-+) Calculations use simple semi-major axis (circular orbits).
  • (-) Plane changes are not calculated (but can be provided by specific overrides).
  • (+) There's a small percentage buffer on non-aero landings to help the numbers line up with DV maps.
For the most part, based on the tests in comparison to other maps and calculators, you are more likely to have a slight overestimate (up to ~10% at most) than any significant underestimate. If in any doubt whatsoever, add MOAR BOOSTERS.
